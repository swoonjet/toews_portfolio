UNMAS × Critical Mass

Sweeper

This installation is deeply personal and emotional, highlighting the devastating impact of landmines. The virtual minefield exhibit at the New Museum in New York City brought this reality to life. The Sweeper mobile app used iBeacon technology to trigger audio explosions on visitors' phones as they navigated the exhibit, followed by stories of the damage caused and calls to donate to UNMAS.

City of Toronto

The Inaccessible Transit Shelter

On behalf of the Centre for Independent Living in Toronto and the City of Toronto, this transit shelter was entirely enclosed in glass so people couldn't get inside. A headline explained: "Feeling left out? Over 400,000 Torontonians with disabilities do, too." A call to action drove people to a website where they could explore ways of making Toronto more accessible.
